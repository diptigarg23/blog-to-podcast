# PRD
## Blog to Podcast Platform

### Product Strategy & Vision

**Product Vision**: Enable knowledge workers to consume Forrester research content through audio (podcast), increasing accessibility and knowledge retention.

**Value Proposition**: 
- Save time: Listen on the go rather than reading
- Increase accessibility (e.g., for visually impaired or preferring audio)
- Enable audio learning for better retention during commutes or workouts

### Target Users

1. **Busy Executives and Professionals**
   - Prefer audio consumption during commutes or workouts
   - Need quick access to insights without reading time
   - Value convenience and time efficiency

### Feature Prioritization

#### MVP Features (Phase 1)
1. ✅ URL input for Forrester blog posts
2. ✅ Text-to-speech podcast generation
4. ✅ Simple UI with download options
5. ✅ Legal attribution and disclaimer

### Success Metrics & KPIs

#### User Experience Metrics
- **Processing Time**: Target < 30 seconds for typical article
- **Success Rate**: Target 95%+ for valid URLs
- **User Satisfaction**: Intuitive interface, clear feedback

#### Quality Metrics
- **Audio Quality**: High-quality, natural-sounding speech
- **Content Accuracy**: Proper extraction and attribution

#### Business Metrics (Hypothetical)
- **User Adoption**: Number of articles processed

### Legal & Compliance Considerations

#### Key Policies Addressed
1. **Forrester Terms of Use**: Content attribution and fair use
2. **Citation Policy**: Proper attribution and source links
3. **Research Usage Policy**: Educational/portfolio use only

#### Risk Mitigation
- Prominent legal disclaimer
- Automatic attribution generation
- Source URL preservation
- Terms acknowledgment option
- Content usage tracking

#### Compliance Features
- ✅ Automatic attribution display
- ✅ Legal disclaimer in UI
- ✅ Source URL preservation
- ✅ Educational use statement
- ✅ Terms of use acknowledgment

#### Reliability Features

#### Performance Optimizations

### Lessons Learned

1. **Legal Considerations**: Important to address compliance from the start
2. **User Flexibility**: Multiple input methods increase usability
3. **Simplicity**: Using a single, reliable TTS engine simplifies the codebase and user experience
4. **Open-Source Benefits**: Easier to share and demonstrate
